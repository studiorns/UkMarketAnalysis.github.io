Data Summary

This dashboard visualizes monthly flight search data, travel queries, hotel guests, and media impressions for Armenia from January 2023 to December 2024. The line graphs display the trends over time, allowing for easy comparison between years and metrics. The data shows significant growth across all metrics in 2024 compared to 2023, particularly in hotel guests and travel queries.

Key Insights & Observations

Seasonal Patterns

All metrics show clear seasonal patterns with peaks during different periods:

  • Flight searches peak in January and September-October
  • Hotel guests reach highest numbers in August-September
  • Travel queries show strongest performance in July-August
  • Media impressions are highest in October, aligning with increased flight searches

This suggests different planning horizons for travelers, with flight searches happening earlier in the year and actual visits occurring in summer/early fall.

Year-over-Year Growth

2024 showed remarkable growth across all metrics compared to 2023:

  • Hotel guests saw the most dramatic increases, with July showing a 308% YoY growth
  • Flight searches in January 2024 were 237% higher than January 2023
  • Travel queries more than doubled across most months
  • Brand consideration improved by 18.75%, while awareness showed modest 2% growth

This consistent growth across all metrics indicates Armenia is gaining significant traction as a travel destination.

Media Impact Analysis

The relationship between media impressions and other metrics reveals interesting patterns:

  • The slight negative correlation (-0.12) between flight searches and impressions suggests media campaigns may not be optimally timed with search behavior
  • October 2024 saw the highest media impressions (28.6M) followed by September (11.3M), which aligns with increased travel queries and flight searches in those months
  • The 2025 planned impressions show a more balanced distribution throughout the year, which may help drive more consistent travel interest
  • The significant increase in planned impressions for Q1 2025 (previously at zero) indicates a strategic shift to capture early-year travel planning

Strategic Recommendations

Based on the data analysis, several strategic opportunities emerge:

  • Shift some media spend from October to January-February to better align with peak flight search periods
  • Focus on converting the high brand awareness (36%) to higher consideration and intent metrics
  • Leverage the strong August-September performance to extend the travel season into October-November
  • Consider targeted campaigns during the February-March period when travel interest begins to rise but hasn't yet peaked
  • Monitor the effectiveness of the planned 2025 media distribution to determine if the more balanced approach yields better results

Optimizing Media Allocation Based on Lead Time

With an average lead time of 42 days between booking and arrival, media impressions should be strategically timed:

  • Early Awareness (60-90 days out): Allocate 30% of impressions to build destination awareness, particularly important given the relatively low 36% awareness metric
  • Active Consideration (30-60 days out): Allocate 50% of impressions during this critical decision-making window, aligning with the 42-day average lead time
  • Booking Conversion (15-30 days out): Allocate 20% of impressions with strong calls-to-action and limited-time offers to drive final booking decisions

Addressing Low Brand Awareness

With brand awareness at only 36%, there's significant opportunity for growth:

  • Expand Digital Footprint: Increase presence on travel planning platforms and social media channels with visually compelling content showcasing Armenia's unique experiences
  • Influencer Partnerships: Collaborate with travel influencers to reach new audiences, particularly focusing on neighboring regions where awareness may be higher
  • Content Marketing Strategy: Develop a year-round content calendar highlighting seasonal attractions to maintain consistent visibility
  • Cross-Promotion: Partner with airlines and regional tourism boards for joint campaigns that leverage their existing audience
  • SEO Optimization: Enhance digital presence for key search terms related to regional travel, cultural experiences, and specific attractions
